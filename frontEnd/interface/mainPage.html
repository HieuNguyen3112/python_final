About Our Team
The Veterinary Anatomy Viewer is a VR anatomy learning experience for canine, equine, and bovine models. It has been developed by the ARIES Team at the Virginia Tech Library, led by Dr. Todd Ogle. Contributions have been made by Lucas Freeman, Zac Kim, Karina Springer, Sam Lally, Nayha Pochiraju, Nicole Muller, Diana Ofori - Manu, and Sam Williams.
For more information about this project, contact Principal Investigator Dr. Michael Nappier, DVM, MAEd, DABVP.
